Meet the Penguins

1. Emperor Penguin

The largest penguin species, known for their majestic size and endurance in freezing temperatures.

2. King Penguin

Recognizable by their vibrant orange patches, these penguins are the second largest species.

3. Little Blue Penguin

The smallest penguin species, often called fairy penguins due to their adorable appearance.

4. Rockhopper Penguin

Named for their habit of hopping among rocky shores, these penguins are known for their spiky feathers.

5. Adelie Penguin

A classic Antarctic penguin, easily identifiable by the white ring around their eyes.

6. Chinstrap Penguin

Distinguished by the black line under their chin, giving the appearance of a helmet strap.
